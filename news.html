Bangladesh Roblox Community Reaches 5,000+ Members Milestone

What started as a small group has grown into one of the largest regional Roblox communities in South Asia.

Admin Team

December 15, 2024 · 5 min read

The Bangladesh Roblox Community has officially crossed the 5,000 member milestone on Discord, marking a significant achievement for our growing family.

"When we started this community in 2022, we never imagined it would grow this fast," said one of the founding admins. "Seeing Bangladeshi talent shine on the global Roblox platform has been incredibly rewarding."

The community has been instrumental in helping new developers learn Roblox Studio, connecting experienced creators for collaborations, and organizing events that have produced award-winning games.

To celebrate, the community is hosting a week-long festival including a special game jam, Q&A sessions with top creators, and exclusive role giveaways.
